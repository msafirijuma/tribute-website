Our Heroes

Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist, widely acknowledged to be one of the greatest physicists of all time. Einstein is best known for his theory of relativity, while he also made an important contribution to the development of the quantum mechanics.

↓

Stephen Hawking

Stephen Hawking was an English theoretical physicist, cosmologist and author whose theory of exploding black holes drew upon both relativity theory and quantum mechanism, He, at the time of his death was director for Theoretical Cosmology at the Universiry Of Cambridge.

↓

Steve Jobs

Steven Paul"Steve" Jobs was an American Information technology enterpreneur and inventor. He was the cofounder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc, CEO and largest shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios, he also was founder, chairman, and CEO of the NeXT Inc. He widely considered a visionary and a genius, he oversaw the launch of revolutionary products as the iPod and iPhone.

↓

Isaac Newton

Sir Isaac Newton was an English mathematician, physicist, astronomer, alchemist, theologian, and author widely recognised as one of the greatest mathematicians and physicist of all time and among the most famous scientist to exist. Isaac Newton is well known for his formulation of the three laws of motion which serve as the basic principle of modern physics.

↓

Michael Faraday

Michael Faraday was an English scientist who is well know for his contribution to the study of electromagnetism and electrochemistry. Michael Faraday's inventions include the electric motor, the transformer, and the electric dynamo or generator. Among the most interesting facts about him is, was able to discover the electrolysis when he was only 20 years old.
